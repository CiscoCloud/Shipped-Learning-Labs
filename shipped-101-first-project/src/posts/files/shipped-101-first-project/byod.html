To work on this lab on your own machine, you will need to a machine and operating system compatible with Vagrant, and Virtual Box. While Vagrant supports Mac OS X, Windows, and many flavors of Unix, in order to ensure the best compatibility with our initial Alpha release of Shipped, we suggest you run Mac OS x.

Hardware Requirements and Recommendations

  • Total Memory: Required: 4 GB, We recommend 8 GB
  • Available Storage: Required: 20 GB free, We recommend 50 GB of Disk Storage free
